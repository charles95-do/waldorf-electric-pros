About Waldorf Electric Pros

Our Story

Waldorf Electric Pros was founded on a simple belief: homeowners and businesses in Southern Maryland deserve an electrician they can actually trust. No runaround on pricing, no sloppy work, no ghosting after the job is done.

We started as a small crew of licensed electricians who grew up in Charles County and wanted to serve our community the right way. Every member of our team is background-checked, drug-tested, and holds the proper Maryland electrical licenses for the work they perform.

Over the years, we've grown through word-of-mouth — the highest compliment in this business. Our customers refer their friends, neighbors, and family because they know we'll treat them with the same respect and quality workmanship.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted electrical contractors in Waldorf, MD. From simple outlet repairs to complex commercial buildouts, every job gets our full attention and our best work.
